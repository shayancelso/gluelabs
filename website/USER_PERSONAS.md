# User Personas & Workflow Patterns

This document defines the target users of the Glue platform, their daily workflows, pain points, and interaction patterns with our tools. Understanding these personas guides feature development and UX decisions.

## Primary User Personas

### 1. Account Manager / Customer Success Manager (CSM)

#### Profile & Background
- **Role**: Day-to-day relationship management and account growth
- **Experience**: 2-8 years in customer success or account management
- **Team Size**: Manages 20-150 accounts depending on segment
- **Technical Skill**: Moderate - comfortable with tools but not technical
- **Goals**: Drive expansion revenue, prevent churn, maintain strong relationships

#### Daily Workflow Pattern

**Morning (8-10 AM): Account Health Check**
```
9:00 AM - Coffee + Account Health Dashboard review
        - Check overnight alerts and notifications
        - Identify accounts needing immediate attention
        - Review usage trends and engagement scores

9:20 AM - Prioritization using Account Health data
        - Red/yellow accounts get priority slots
        - Review upcoming renewal dates
        - Check for support ticket escalations

9:45 AM - Email and Slack catch-up
        - Customer outreach planning
        - Internal team coordination
```

**Mid-Morning (10-12 PM): Deep Analysis**
```
10:00 AM - Whitespace Visualizer session (2-3 accounts)
         - Identify expansion opportunities
         - Quantify potential revenue impact
         - Prepare talking points for customer calls

11:00 AM - CRM updates and call preparation
         - Log findings from analysis
         - Schedule follow-up activities
         - Prepare meeting agendas
```

**Afternoon (1-5 PM): Customer Interaction**
```
1:00 PM - Customer calls and meetings
        - Present expansion opportunities
        - Discuss account health concerns
        - Gather feedback and requirements

3:00 PM - Follow-up and documentation
        - Update CRM with meeting notes
        - Create action items and timelines
        - Schedule next touchpoints

4:00 PM - Pipeline and forecast updates
        - Update expansion opportunity stages
        - Adjust revenue projections
        - Prepare reports for management
```

#### Primary Pain Points
1. **Data Fragmentation**: "I spend 2 hours every morning just gathering data from different systems"
2. **Manual Analysis**: "By the time I finish analyzing opportunities, I have no time left for customer calls"
3. **Reactive Mode**: "I only find out about problems when customers are already unhappy"
4. **Opportunity Blindness**: "I know there's more revenue potential, but I can't see where"
5. **Inconsistent Process**: "Every analysis feels like starting from scratch"

#### Tool Usage Patterns

**Whitespace Visualizer (Primary Tool)**
- **Frequency**: 3-5 times per week
- **Session Duration**: 15-30 minutes per account
- **Use Cases**: 
  - Quarterly account reviews
  - Expansion opportunity identification
  - Customer meeting preparation
- **Key Features Used**: Matrix view, opportunity scoring, playbook generation

**Account Health Dashboard (When Available)**
- **Frequency**: Daily morning check
- **Session Duration**: 5-10 minutes
- **Use Cases**: 
  - Morning prioritization routine
  - Alert-based investigation
  - Trend monitoring

#### Success Metrics & KPIs
- **Expansion Revenue**: $50K-$200K per quarter
- **Account Growth Rate**: 15-25% year-over-year
- **Customer Health Score**: Maintain >75% of accounts in green
- **Activity Efficiency**: Reduce analysis time by 60%

### 2. VP of Customer Success / Revenue (Leadership)

#### Profile & Background
- **Role**: Strategic oversight and team performance management
- **Experience**: 8-20 years, mix of customer success and sales leadership
- **Team Size**: 5-25 CSMs and support staff
- **Technical Skill**: High-level understanding, delegates technical work
- **Goals**: Predictable revenue growth, team scalability, operational efficiency

#### Weekly Workflow Pattern

**Monday: Week Planning & Review**
```
9:00 AM - Team performance dashboard review
        - Individual CSM metrics and trends
        - Pipeline health and forecast accuracy
        - Customer satisfaction trends

10:00 AM - Leadership team meeting
         - Present customer success metrics
         - Discuss cross-functional initiatives
         - Review quarterly progress

11:00 AM - Team 1:1s and coaching
         - Individual performance discussions
         - Opportunity prioritization guidance
         - Process improvement feedback
```

**Wednesday: Strategic Analysis**
```
9:00 AM - Aggregate Whitespace Analysis
        - Review team's expansion pipeline
        - Identify cross-account patterns
        - Spot market opportunities

10:30 AM - Forecast refinement
         - Update quarterly revenue projections
         - Adjust team targets and quotas
         - Risk assessment for key accounts

12:00 PM - Executive reporting preparation
         - Compile metrics for board deck
         - Create success stories and case studies
         - Prepare strategic recommendations
```

**Friday: Process Optimization**
```
2:00 PM - Tool effectiveness review
        - Analyze team tool usage patterns
        - Identify training needs
        - Evaluate ROI on current solutions

3:00 PM - Best practice development
        - Document successful approaches
        - Plan team training sessions
        - Refine standard operating procedures
```

#### Primary Pain Points
1. **Visibility Gaps**: "I don't know which CSM is using which approach - no consistency"
2. **Forecast Accuracy**: "Revenue predictions are all over the place, can't plan hiring"
3. **Scalability Issues**: "Every new hire needs 3 months of training to be productive"
4. **ROI Uncertainty**: "We're buying tools but I can't prove they're working"
5. **Manual Reporting**: "I spend my Friday afternoons building slides instead of strategizing"

#### Tool Usage Patterns

**Aggregate Dashboard Views**
- **Frequency**: Daily high-level check, weekly deep dive
- **Session Duration**: 10-45 minutes
- **Use Cases**: Team performance monitoring, trend identification, forecasting

**Whitespace Analysis (Strategic View)**
- **Frequency**: Monthly comprehensive review
- **Session Duration**: 1-2 hours
- **Use Cases**: Market analysis, team training examples, strategic planning

#### Success Metrics & KPIs
- **Team NRR (Net Revenue Retention)**: Target >115%
- **Forecast Accuracy**: Within 5% of quarterly predictions
- **CSM Productivity**: Revenue per CSM growth >20% annually
- **Tool ROI**: Measurable efficiency gains and revenue attribution

### 3. Revenue Operations Analyst (RevOps)

#### Profile & Background
- **Role**: Data analysis, process optimization, and reporting
- **Experience**: 3-10 years in operations, analytics, or consulting
- **Technical Skill**: High - Excel expert, some SQL, process automation
- **Team Size**: Individual contributor or small team (1-3 people)
- **Goals**: Data accuracy, automated insights, process efficiency

#### Daily Workflow Pattern

**Morning: Data Quality & Reporting**
```
8:30 AM - System health check
        - Verify data synchronization across systems
        - Check automated report generation
        - Identify data quality issues

9:30 AM - Analysis requests and ad-hoc reporting
        - CSM team requests for specific insights
        - Executive dashboard updates
        - Customer segmentation analysis

11:00 AM - Process improvement projects
         - Workflow optimization initiatives
         - Tool evaluation and testing
         - Integration development
```

**Afternoon: Strategic Analysis**
```
1:00 PM - Deep-dive analysis sessions
        - Customer behavior pattern identification
        - Expansion opportunity modeling
        - Churn risk factor analysis

3:00 PM - Documentation and training
        - Update process documentation
        - Create training materials for CSM team
        - Develop best practice guides

4:00 PM - Tool maintenance and optimization
        - Configure dashboard alerts
        - Update analysis templates
        - Test new feature implementations
```

#### Primary Pain Points
1. **Manual Data Integration**: "I spend 40% of my time copying data between systems"
2. **Analysis Backlog**: "Requests pile up faster than I can deliver insights"
3. **Tool Limitations**: "Current tools are either too basic or too expensive"
4. **Knowledge Bottleneck**: "I'm the only one who knows how to run these analyses"
5. **Reactive Analytics**: "By the time I finish the analysis, the opportunity is gone"

#### Tool Usage Patterns

**Whitespace Visualizer (Power User)**
- **Frequency**: Daily for analysis requests
- **Session Duration**: 30-60 minutes per analysis
- **Use Cases**: 
  - Custom analysis for executive team
  - CSM training and template creation
  - Market research and competitive analysis
- **Advanced Features**: Custom data import, complex filtering, export capabilities

**Analysis Template Creation**
- **Frequency**: Weekly template updates
- **Session Duration**: 1-2 hours per template
- **Use Cases**: Standardizing CSM analysis approaches, scalable insights

#### Success Metrics & KPIs
- **Analysis Turnaround Time**: <24 hours for standard requests
- **Data Accuracy**: >99% accuracy in automated reports
- **Process Automation**: 50% reduction in manual tasks
- **Team Enablement**: CSM self-service analytics adoption

## Cross-Persona Workflow Intersections

### Daily Team Standup (15 minutes)
```
CSM updates: "Account X has a $50K expansion opportunity - analyzed in Whitespace Visualizer"
RevOps insight: "Seeing pattern across enterprise accounts - 40% adoption = highest expansion rate"
VP coaching: "Focus on accounts with 2+ yellow health scores this week"
```

### Weekly Pipeline Review (30 minutes)
```
VP reviews aggregate expansion pipeline
CSMs present top 3 opportunities with Whitespace analysis
RevOps provides trends and benchmarking data
Team collaborates on approach and resource allocation
```

### Monthly Strategic Planning (2 hours)
```
RevOps presents market analysis and trends
VP sets team priorities based on data insights
CSMs input on customer feedback and market needs
Executive team aligns on quarterly targets
```

## Persona-Driven Design Principles

### For Account Managers
- **Speed over Depth**: Quick insights that enable immediate action
- **Visual Clarity**: Charts and matrices that tell story at a glance
- **Action Orientation**: Always provide next steps and recommendations
- **Context Preservation**: Remember previous analyses and build upon them

### For Leadership 
- **Aggregation Focus**: Team-level and trend views over individual details
- **Forecast Accuracy**: Predictive insights that enable planning
- **ROI Visibility**: Clear connection between tool usage and business outcomes
- **Scalability Evidence**: Proof that processes work across the team

### For Revenue Operations
- **Data Flexibility**: Custom analysis capabilities and export options
- **Process Automation**: Reduce manual tasks through smart defaults
- **Template Creation**: Enable standardization across the team
- **Integration Readiness**: APIs and data formats that connect with existing stack
