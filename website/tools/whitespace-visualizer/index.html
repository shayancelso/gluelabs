Understanding Whitespace

What is whitespace analysis?

The systematic process to identify unmet demand within your existing accounts

Whitespace analysis reveals gaps between what your customers currently buy from you and what they could potentially purchase. It's the strategic method for mapping expansion opportunities across your account portfolio.
